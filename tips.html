Stress Management Tips

Managing stress doesn’t have to be complicated. Here are practical ways to stay calm and balanced.

Practice Breathing Exercises

Inhale deeply, hold, and exhale slowly to calm your body instantly.

Manage Your Time

Break tasks into smaller steps and prioritize what matters most.

Live a Healthy Lifestyle

Eat balanced meals, sleep well, and exercise regularly.

Practice Mindfulness

Spend a few minutes meditating or staying present each day.

Stay Positive

Replace negative thoughts with hopeful ones and stay motivated.

Take Breaks

Give yourself short breaks while studying or working to recharge your mind.
